My Little Buddy — Virtual Pet Simulator

A charming, ASCII-art virtual pet simulator inspired by **Moshi Monsters**, **Tamagotchi**, and **Neopets** that is built entirely in Python with **no external dependencies**!


## Features
- **Gacha Adoption System**: Spin for 1 of 6 unique pets with rarity tiers (Common → Legendary)
- **Care Mechanics**: Feed, play, clean, and sleep with meaningful tradeoffs
- **Personality System**: Each pet has unique traits affecting gameplay
- **3-Stage Evolution**: Evolve through baby → child → adult with branching paths
- **Mini-Games**: Bubble Pop and Memory Match for extra rewards
- **Unlockable Themes**: Forest, Space, Sunset, Night, and Chromatic themes
- **Pet Collection**: Adopt and switch between multiple buddies
- **Daily Bonuses**: Earn currency for consistent care

## Pet Species
| **Fuzzball** | Common | Cuddly hamstercreature |
| **Glitterpup** | Uncommon | Sparkly dog|
| **Slimey** | Common | Bouncy blob |
| **Starwhisker** | Rare | Cosmic cat|
| **Dragonling** | Epic | Baby dragon|
| **Nebulite** | Legendary | Alien space creature |

Each pet evolves based on your care style:
- **Joy Form** (High Happiness)
- **Pure Form** (High Cleanliness)  
- **Plush Form** (Well-Fed)
- **Spark Form** (High Energy)
- **Bonded Form** (High Affection)

## Installation
Just download and run the .exe!

## Starting Resources
- **50 Buddy Bucks** 
- **1 Free Gacha Roll** 

Use your free roll to adopt your first buddy, then earn more currency through:
- Filling Satisfaction meter (+30BB)
- Evolving pets (+100BB)
- Playing mini-games (up to +20BB)

## Visual Design
All graphics are rendered using **AI GENERATION** with:
- **Idle animations**: Breathing and blinking
- **Action frames**: Eating, playing, sleeping
- **Evolution effects**: Sparkles and special forms
- **Themed colors**: Dynamic UI that matches your selected theme

## Save System

- **Global save**: Currency, unlocked themes, collection
- **Per-pet saves**: Individual stats, evolution stage, personality
- **Save location**: `saves/` folder (created automatically)
-   Use rm -rf saves/ to start over

## Controls

- **Mouse**: Click action buttons and pet your buddy
- **Menu Bar**: Access adoption, mini-games, and settings
- **Keyboard**: Standard window controls (Alt+F4 to close)

## Unlockable Themes

| Theme | Unlock Condition |
|-------|------------------|
| **Forest** | Default |
| **Space** | Evolve 2 pets |
| **Sunset** | Fill Satisfaction meter 10 times |
| **Night** | Play at 10 PM or later |
| **Chromatic** | Obtain a Legendary pet |


### Mini-games not working
- Make sure you've adopted a pet first
- Mini-games require an active buddy to play
